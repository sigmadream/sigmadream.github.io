- [The Growing Problem of Bots That Fight Online](https://www.technologyreview.com/s/602421/the-growing-problem-of-bots-that-fight-online/)

  - 웹에서 사용되는 Bot은 어떻게 상호작용 하는지를 위키백과를 사용해서 연구한 내용을 소개하고 있음
  - 그 중에서도 위키백과를 수정하는 과정에서 Bot과 Bot의 충돌이 났을 때 발생하는 문제를 어떻게 해야 하는지 대해서 간략하고 소개하고 있고, 자세한 사항은 [논문](https://arxiv.org/ftp/arxiv/papers/1609/1609.04285.pdf)을 참고하자!
  - >
  - And their impact is growing. By some measures, bots account for 49 percent of visits to Web pages and are responsible for over 50 percent of clicks on ads. This impact is set to increase as the number of bots rises exponentially.

- [Google's New Vacation App Was 280 Years In The Making](https://www.wired.com/2016/09/googles-new-vacation-app-280-years-making/)
  - 구글에서 최근 발표한 여행앱에 사용된 알고리즘과 관련된 간단한 내용을 소개하고 있음
  - 시간이 허락한다면 [Euler](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eulerian_path), [TSP](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Travelling_salesman_problem), [Christofides algorithm](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christofides_algorithm) 등의 내용에도 관심을 가져보자!
  - >
  - “If you know the places you want to visit, you can use algorithms built on top of Euler to figure out the best route,” says Google researcher Andrew Tomkins, who worked on the project. “Euler is a sub-routine for our itinerary work.” [...] “We want to pool the collective wisdom.”
